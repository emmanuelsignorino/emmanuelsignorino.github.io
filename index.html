About Me

Born in Cannes, Emmanuel Signorino is a creator of art in all its forms. Using and mixing all techniques, from acrylic paint to digital painting on iPad. His multiple universe is made up of dreams, imaginary creatures, female portraits. A adventurer of art, he does not impose any particular style and passes from Street-art to figurative and abstract.
